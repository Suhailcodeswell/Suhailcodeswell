# Suhail Ahmed

Software developer and business analyst. I like questions that start with "I wonder if"
and end with something you can actually use: a pipeline, a model, a dashboard, or a
website that settles the argument.

Most of my work sits where business questions meet data. I clean it, model it, query it,
and then build the thing that explains it to people who don't read SQL.

## Featured work

**[Trump Posts & Financial Markets](https://github.com/Suhailcodeswell/trump-post-market-analysis)**
Do Trump's posts move markets? An event study of 73,380 posts measured against Bitcoin,
oil, the S&P 500, and the Nasdaq. Python pipeline, SQL star schema, a Power BI report,
and a live website with an investment calculator.

**[Employee Attrition Prediction System](https://github.com/Suhailcodeswell/Employee-Attrition-Prediction-System)**
A machine learning model that predicts employee attrition with 83% recall, projected to
save over $315K a year in replacement costs.

**[Telco Customer Analytics](https://github.com/Suhailcodeswell/Telco_Customer_Analytics)**
SQL analysis of telecom customer churn with predictive risk scoring.

**[Holiday Inventory Optimization](https://github.com/Suhailcodeswell/Holiday-Inventory-Optimization-via-Demand-Forecasting)**
Demand forecasting applied to the messiest retail problem there is: how much stock to
hold for the holidays.
